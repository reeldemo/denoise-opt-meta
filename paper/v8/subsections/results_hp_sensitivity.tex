\paragraph{Hyperparameter sensitivity (Q3).}
\label{para:hp-sensitivity}
RQ: how sensitive is hybrid champion $R$ to $\pm 50\%$ shifts of Table~\ref{tab:hyperparams} knobs?
We run a \textbf{one-at-a-time sensitivity probe} (not a full $5$k re-search): $500$ outer iterations per config, seed \texttt{1902771841}, sine{+}cliff FitCell matched to the meta-compare protocol (batch $48$, fit steps $24$).
Perturbed knobs: population size $n$, PPO clip $\epsilon$, fit Adam learning rate, GA second-mutate rate, and PPO entropy coefficient.
Table~\ref{tab:hp-sensitivity} and Figure~\ref{fig:hp-sensitivity} report champion absolute $R$ versus the Table-2 default (default champ $R\approx 0.9888$; largest $|\Delta R|$ among completed arms $\approx 0.0123$ on \texttt{lr\_m50}).
All $11/11$ arms completed under this budget.

This is local robustness evidence under the stated budget and seed; the $5$k matched-budget ranking (Table~\ref{tab:meta-approaches}) remains the primary approach comparison.
We do \emph{not} claim that Table-2 defaults are globally optimal---only that $\pm 50\%$ OAT shifts leave champion $R$ within a small band relative to the DualCosine gap ($\approx 0.17$ on the $5$k gate).
