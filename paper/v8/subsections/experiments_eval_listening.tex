\paragraph{Audible demos and vibrato spectrograms.}
Downloadable wavetable clips (no-bake / DualCosine / Ours) live under
\texttt{reelsynth/brand/artifacts/meta\_approach\_compare/hear\_samples/}
($44.1$\,kHz mono, A4, $3$\,s; rebuild: \texttt{scripts/export\_meta\_hear\_samples.py}).
A slow-vibrato spectrogram protocol (\texttt{scripts/bench\_vibrato\_spectrogram.py})
scores the same bake cells under modulated-pitch playback; figures appear in Section~\ref{sec:vibrato-eval}.
These assets support informal audition and spectrogram inspection only---formal listening panels are out of scope (Section~\ref{sec:limitations}).
